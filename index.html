About io.LOL

You can explore the world, play with friends, find food to level up your character, and compete with others!

How to Play

Use your left mouse button to move forward and control the direction with your mouse. Press space to sprint. Eat food to get bigger. Avoid bigger players, and attack smaller ones.

Animals

We plan on adding many more new animals in the future, so check back frequently to play as new characters! Keep in mind, the game is still only in the prototype stage.
